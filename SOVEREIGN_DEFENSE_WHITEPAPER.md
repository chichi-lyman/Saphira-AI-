## The Architectural Bottleneck of the "Static Edge"

*   **The Workflow:** Drone feed -> Model Inference -> Alert Operator -> Operator Action.
*   **The Flaw:** This "human-in-the-loop" requirement introduces fatal latency. Against a peer-adversary employing swarm tactics or autonomous cyber-warfare, the perception-to-action cycle is too slow.

While no-code model tuning allows for rapid adaptation to new hardware, the resulting intelligence remains task-specific. A model trained to recognize an enemy transport cannot write a script to jam its communications.
